# AI Content Disclosure — *Talosapien*

For KDP's "Did you use AI tools to create content?" question, and for the author's own record.

## KDP form answer
- **AI-generated text/images/translations used?** Yes.
- **Category per KDP definitions:** "AI-generated" text content, with **AI-assisted edits** and
  substantial human direction, structuring, curation, and revision; the human author is responsible
  for the final work. The cover image is generated programmatically (a deterministic Python script,
  not a generative-image model); disclose per current KDP policy at time of upload.

## Honest statement (may be adapted for a colophon)
*Talosapien* was written by an AI system under the direction and editorship of its human publisher,
working as the pen name **Otto Quill**. A human set the brief, made the binding creative decisions,
named the characters, curated and revised the text, and bears final responsibility for the published
work. The drafting, the world bible, and much of the prose were generated by the AI, including
per-character drafting instances used to make the cast's behavior more truthful. We disclose this
plainly because the novel's own subject—what it means, and what it costs, to hand a civilization's
hardest work to a capable and unreadable machine—runs directly through the manner of its making.

## Note on training data / rights
The manuscript is original. It is in conversation with the scientists and philosophers named in the
back-matter bibliography but quotes no copyrighted contemporary text; brief quotations, where present,
are from public-domain sources and are attributed. The work is published **All Rights Reserved** and
