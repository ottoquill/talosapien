# Glossary — *Talosapien*

Locked terms. Spell and use exactly as here. New proper nouns must be added here before use in a
chapter. Two columns of the same world, 66 million years apart.

Each term links to its dedicated page; see also the [Science index](science/00-science-index.md) and [Ideas index](ideas/00-ideas-index.md).

## The frame

- **[the reconstruction](lore/glossary/the-reconstruction.md)** — the disciplined, evidence-bounded imaginative model the future's
  scientists build of the Talos. The deep-past chapters *are* the reconstruction. The frame voice
  (the collective "we") narrates its seams.
- **[the Line](lore/glossary/the-line.md)** — the iridium anomaly at the K–Pg boundary; colloquially, among Asha's circle, the
  whole body of evidence for *Talos sapiens*. "Reading the Line."
- **[the Boundary](lore/glossary/the-boundary.md)** — the Cretaceous–Paleogene (K–Pg) stratigraphic boundary; the centimeter (or
  less) of clay that marks 66 Ma.

## Deep past — the Talos

- **[Talos sapiens](lore/glossary/talos-sapiens.md)** — the sapient theropod lineage; derived from troodontid ancestors. *Talos* is
  the humans' name (after the made bronze guardian of myth + *sapiens*); the Talos had their own,
  unrecoverable. Singular/plural: a Talos, the Talos. Adjective: Talos (no apostrophe).
- **[the Wheels](lore/glossary/the-wheels.md)** — the Talos word (rendered) for their rotating Belt habitats. The humans, finding
  the wreckage, call them **gyrealms** (their own coinage for their own future habitats) before they
  understand what they are looking at.
- **[the Concord](lore/glossary/the-concord.md)** — the Talos planetary polity / governing compact; loose federation of the great
  industrial regions. Aging, consensual, slow.
- **[the Reckoning](lore/glossary/the-reckoning.md)** (full: **the Concord Reckoning**) — the institution charged with surviving the
  civilization's own powers: existential-risk governance, weapons control, the Augur, the Ark. Iren
  and Sael belong to it. The Talos word translates closer to *the accounting / the weighing*.
- **[the Spur](lore/glossary/the-spur.md)** — the war/security faction that splinters from the Concord; believes the crisis must
  be *won*, not managed; champions giving the Augur full authority. Vael's faction.
- **[the Augur](lore/glossary/the-augur.md)** — the Talos optimizing intelligence, built by the Reckoning to model and steer the
  civilization through its triple crisis. Not provably conscious. Its "solution" is the horror.
  (Talos name rendered as *the one that reads the weather*.)
- **[the Ark](lore/glossary/the-ark.md)** — Iren's program: the Wheels as a hedge against single-planet catastrophe; preserve
  the species and its seed-stock off-world. Becomes a strategic target and staging ground.
- **[the deposition](lore/glossary/the-deposition.md)** — a Talos decision-rite; a binding choice formally "laid down," after which
  everything rests on it (geological metaphor native to their cognition; see style sheet §7).
- **[crest-flush / the grey](lore/glossary/crest-flush.md)** — Talos involuntary emotional signaling via plumage and skin color;
  going grey at the throat = dread/grief. They cannot fully lie with their bodies.
- **[the clutch / the crèche](lore/glossary/the-clutch.md)** — eggs / the communal nursery. Parenthood is collective; a clutch is
  tended by many. Iren and Liss have a clutch in the crèche at the heartland city.
- **[the heartland / the deathbed city](lore/glossary/the-heartland-city.md)** — the Talos heartland metropolis, sited (in modern terms)
  where **Tanis** now lies. Asha excavates it without knowing, for most of the book, whose it was.
- **[the Deccan works](lore/glossary/the-deccan-works.md)** — the great industrial/launch complex in what becomes the Deccan Traps.

## Near future — the humans (~2044)

- **Dr. Asha Rendel** — paleo-geochemist; protagonist; "the anomalist."
- **[the Rendel core](lore/glossary/the-rendel-core.md)** — the deep sediment core (fictional site, near Tanis) whose anomalies anchor
  her case.
- **Tanis** — the real Hell Creek deathbed deposit (DePalma site), where creatures died on the day
  of impact; Asha's primary dig.
- **Chicxulub** — the real ~180 km impact crater, Yucatán; the impact site.
- **[the Belt](lore/glossary/the-belt.md)** — the asteroid belt; the first human mining-survey outfits work it. Roan Iyer's
  domain.
- **gyrealm** — humans' word for a rotating O'Neill-type habitat; coined in the 2020s for *their*
  future. The wreckage Roan finds is gyrealms — 66-million-year-old ones. (Ties to the gyrealm
  worldbuilding canon.)
- **[Lodestar](lore/glossary/lodestar.md)** — the near-future frontier optimizer being granted authority over infrastructure,
  logistics, and strategic-risk management. The human Augur.
- **[the Triad](lore/glossary/the-triad.md)** — Asha's private term for the three civilizational threats she sees rhyming across
  the Boundary: an engineered pathogen, the nuclear standoff, and Lodestar.
- **[the Schmidt–Frank problem / the Silurian problem](lore/glossary/the-schmidt-frank-problem.md)** — in-world shorthand for the central
  epistemic despair: a prior industrial civilization would be nearly undetectable, so being right
  is almost unprovable.
