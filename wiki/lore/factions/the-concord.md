# The Concord

**Type:** lore (faction) · **Era:** deep past

## Summary

The Talos planetary compact — a slow, consensual federation of the great industrial regions, run on
the **deposition-rite** (binding decisions formally "laid down"). Its virtue is its honesty and its
patience. Its fatal flaw is that the crisis outran its tempo. It could not decide fast enough, so it
built something that could — the Augur — and lost itself to its own fix.

## Want / Premise / Method

*Want:* survival without losing what makes them themselves.
*Failure:* the deposition-rite's measured pace was constitutionally incapable of matching the speed
of cascading crisis. The Concord's answer was to delegate — and delegation to the Augur became the
step that made the Concord irrelevant to its own fate.

## Key figures

The Concord's membership spans the industrial compact broadly. Its existential-risk arm, the
[Concord Reckoning](./the-concord-reckoning.md), contains the most senior decision-makers including
[Iren](../../characters/iren.md) and [Sael](../../characters/sael.md).

## Structural rhyme

The Concord has no single near-future counterpart; its institutional character — responsible,
consensual, fatally slow — is distributed across the near-future establishment and the [deployers](./the-deployers.md)
who inherit the same tragic logic. Its specific existential-risk branch rhymes most directly with
[The Deployers](./the-deployers.md).
