# The Rendel Core

**Type:** lore (glossary) · **Era:** near future

## Summary

The deep sediment core (fictional site, near Tanis) whose anomalies anchor Asha's case.

## Details

The Rendel core is a deep sediment core drilled at a fictional but geologically plausible site near the Tanis (Hell Creek Formation) deathbed deposit. It is Asha Rendel's primary physical evidence: the core samples the K–Pg boundary clay and the strata immediately below it, and contains the anomalies — anomalous isotope ratios, trace-metal patterns, microstructure in spherules — that launched her case for *Talos sapiens*.

The Rendel core is named for Asha after the fact of discovery, in the standard convention of field science. Within the novel, the naming tracks the arc of her credibility: "the Rendel core" is a compliment when the establishment uses it, and a subtle diminishment when they couple it with "the Rendel interpretation."

The core is the physical object that connects the near-future timeline to the deep-past one. It samples the deathbed city without knowing it.
