# Talos sapiens

**Type:** lore (glossary) · **Era:** deep past

## Summary

The sapient theropod lineage; derived from troodontid ancestors. *Talos* is the humans' name (after the made bronze guardian of myth + *sapiens*); the Talos had their own name, unrecoverable. Singular/plural: a Talos, the Talos. Adjective: Talos (no apostrophe).

## Details

The Talos arose and built across the final ~2–3 million years of the Maastrichtian — a civilization fast and bright and short. They kept the body plan of their troodontid ancestors: bipedal, feathered, tailed, light-boned. Sapience did not make them into humans. It made them into themselves, more so.

Key biological traits established in the bible:

- **Size and frame:** roughly human-mass but lighter; digitigrade, counterbalanced by a stiffened tail; three grasping fingers per hand with true opposition sufficient for fine tools.
- **Plumage:** feathered with a mobile crest and throat; patterned in the near-ultraviolet. Their world has colors the prose gestures toward and never names in human terms.
- **Vision:** tetrachromatic with a fourth near-UV cone; acute motion and edge detection (predator inheritance).
- **Crest-flush:** an involuntary chromatophore-and-plumage emotional channel. They evolved language over a body that cannot fully lie — so their politics, law, and intimacy are built around the management and reading of an honest body.
- **Vestibular acuity:** exquisite sense of balance and rotation, which is why the spinning Wheels feel uncannily right to them.
- **Reproduction:** oviparous, communal; eggs laid into a shared crèche tended collectively.

The name "Talos sapiens" is the human scientific coinage. The Talos had their own name for themselves, unrecoverable by any method available to the Reconstruction.
