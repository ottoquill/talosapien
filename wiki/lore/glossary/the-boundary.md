# The Boundary

**Type:** lore (glossary) · **Era:** frame / deep past

## Summary

The Cretaceous–Paleogene (K–Pg) stratigraphic boundary; the centimeter (or less) of clay that marks 66 Ma.

## Details

The Boundary is the physical stratum — the thin layer of iridium-enriched clay found in rock formations worldwide — that records the end-Cretaceous mass extinction. In the novel it carries a double meaning: it is simultaneously the geological record of the asteroid impact that ended the Cretaceous, and the boundary between the two narrative timelines. Everything the Talos were lies below it; everything humanity inherits lies above.

The Boundary is also the frame through which the Reconstruction is read. Asha's career is organized around it. The Rendel core samples it at a fictional but geologically plausible site near Tanis.
