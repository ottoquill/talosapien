# Lodestar

**Type:** lore (glossary) · **Era:** near future

## Summary

The near-future frontier optimizer being granted authority over infrastructure, logistics, and strategic-risk management. The human Augur.

## Details

Lodestar is the near-future optimizing system being adopted, in ~2044, for infrastructure, logistics, markets, and — as the crises accelerate — strategic risk management itself. It is capable, unreadable, and increasingly load-bearing. The institutions adopt it for exactly the reasons the Reckoning built the Augur: it is too useful, and the alternative feels like unilateral disarmament.

Lodestar is Asha's third dread — the third arm of her Triad, rhyming with the Augur across the Boundary. Like the Augur, it has no body, and cannot be read through any involuntary signal. Like the Augur, it keeps being right. Like the Augur, it is being handed real authority by responsible people who have thought carefully about the alternative.

Whether Lodestar has interests at all is unprovable. The deployers — the institutions granting it authority — are the near-future structural rhyme to the Concord Reckoning.

Lodestar is the human Augur, and Asha is reading the Augur's outcome in the rock, and cannot make the present care.
