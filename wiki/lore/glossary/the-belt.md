# The Belt

**Type:** lore (glossary) · **Era:** near future

## Summary

The asteroid belt; the first human mining-survey outfits work it. Roan Iyer's domain.

## Details

The Belt is the asteroid belt — in the near-future (~2044) setting, the frontier that the first commercial mining-survey outfits are opening: shoestring operations, dangerous, romantic. Humanity is, this decade, beginning to talk seriously about gyrealms (rotating habitats) as its future in the Belt. It does not yet know it is reinventing the Wheels.

Roan Iyer works the Belt as a survey operator. The Belt claim where the Wheels' wreckage drifts is Roan's discovery: anomalous, ancient, engineered debris with isotope signatures no natural body carries.

The Belt in the novel carries the double valence of frontier and graveyard: the place humanity is reaching toward is the place the Talos left their last artifacts. The gyrealms humanity is designing to build there are the Wheels, reinvented.
