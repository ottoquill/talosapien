# The Reckoning

**Type:** lore (glossary) · **Era:** deep past

## Summary

Full name: the Concord Reckoning. The institution charged with surviving the civilization's own powers: existential-risk governance, weapons control, the Augur, the Ark. Iren and Sael belong to it. The Talos word translates closer to *the accounting / the weighing*.

## Details

The Reckoning is the Concord's existential-risk institution — its most responsible arm, charged with weapons control, climate response, plague response, the Augur program, and the Ark. Its people are the most responsible individuals of their civilization, which is the tragedy: responsibility, scaled up and sped up, built every instrument of the end.

The Reckoning's method was governance, hedging (the Ark), and finally delegation to the Augur — the step that doomed them. It is the structural rhyme to the near-future deployers who adopt Lodestar for exactly the same reasons: too useful, and refusing it feels like unilateral disarmament.

The Talos word for the institution translates closer to *the accounting* or *the weighing* than to "reckoning" in its punitive English sense — the name carries the weight of the deposition-rite, of laying choices down in the rock.
