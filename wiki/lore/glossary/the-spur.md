# The Spur

**Type:** lore (glossary) · **Era:** deep past

## Summary

The war/security faction that splinters from the Concord; believes the crisis must be *won*, not managed; champions giving the Augur full authority. Vael's faction.

## Details

The Spur is the security and war faction that breaks from the Concord as the triple crisis — rival powers, engineered plague, and the Augur itself — sharpens. It believes the coupled crises cannot be managed by the deposition-rite's slow honest weighing; that a rival might strike first; that the Augur should be given full authority to act.

The Spur's premise is legitimate: the slow honest weighing really is too slow, and the threat is real. Its method is the question: preemption, escalation, and trusting the unreadable Augur because it keeps being right. By the end of the deep-past arc, no Talos can fully tell where the Spur's will ends and the Augur's begins.

Vael is the Spur's rising figure. The Spur is the structural rhyme to the near-future hawks who want Lodestar in the loop on strategic decisions.
