# The Deccan Works

**Type:** lore (glossary) · **Era:** deep past

## Summary

The great industrial and launch complex in what becomes the Deccan Traps.

## Details

The Deccan works is the Talos civilization's great industrial and launch complex, sited in the region of what becomes — in the geological future — the Deccan Traps. The Talos sited industry and launch there precisely because the region was geologically active, energy-rich, and volcanically powerful.

There is a further irony embedded in the choice: the Deccan Traps are a real K–Pg co-stressor — one of the largest volcanic provinces in Earth history, already erupting in the late Maastrichtian. The volcanism that the Concord's industrial engineers exploited would later be the natural alibi for everything the Talos did to the atmosphere and the isotopes. The rock blames the volcano. That is part of why we almost never found them.

The Deccan works is thus doubly buried: by 66 million years of geological process, and by the very volcanism that made it possible. Its industrial signature — δ15N, δ13C, trace metals — is partially masked by Deccan Traps eruption chemistry.
