- [kpg-boundary.md](kpg-boundary.md) — the Cretaceous–Paleogene stratigraphic boundary; the centimeter of clay at 66 Ma that the whole novel stands on.

- [chicxulub-crater.md](chicxulub-crater.md) — the ~180 km impact structure in Yucatán; the confirmed source of the K–Pg ejecta layer.

- [microtektites.md](microtektites.md) — impact spherules and microtektites; the boundary clay component whose microstructure Asha argues looks manufactured.
- [impact-winter.md](impact-winter.md) — the kill mechanism: sulfate aerosols, soot, and photosynthesis collapse following the Chicxulub impact.
- [maastrichtian.md](maastrichtian.md) — the final age of the Cretaceous (~72–66 Ma); the temporal setting of the Talos civilisation.

- [geomagnetic-polarity-stratigraphy.md](geomagnetic-polarity-stratigraphy.md) — the magnetic reversal record used to correlate the Line globally.
