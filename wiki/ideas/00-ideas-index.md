# Ideas Index — *Talosapien*

The philosophical and speculative frameworks the novel dramatizes. None are lectured in-text; each
is staged through character and situation (style sheet §5).

---

- [silurian-hypothesis.md](silurian-hypothesis.md) — Schmidt & Frank (2018): could we even detect a prior industrial civilisation? Mostly not — only chemical/isotopic ghosts; Asha's despair-and-method.
- [fermi-paradox.md](fermi-paradox.md) — the silence of the universe given billions of likely worlds; the epilogue's vertigo: we may be the second draft, not the first.
- [great-filter.md](great-filter.md) — Hanson's argument that some catastrophic barrier filters civilisations; the question of whether the filter is behind us or ahead.
- [drake-equation.md](drake-equation.md) — the probabilistic framework for estimating communicating civilisations; the optimism that makes the silence hurt.
- [vulnerable-world-hypothesis.md](vulnerable-world-hypothesis.md) — Bostrom: some technologies are "black balls" a civilisation cannot un-invent and may not survive drawing; the Augur and Lodestar as the urn already open.
- [future-generations-ethics.md](future-generations-ethics.md) — Parfit and Scheffler on what the living owe to those not yet born; Liss's clutch and Asha's warning as a gift to people who don't exist yet.
- [non-identity-problem.md](non-identity-problem.md) — Parfit's puzzle: future people harmed by a policy would not exist without it; the ethical trap around Asha's warning.
- [deep-time.md](deep-time.md) — the geological-timescale intuition (Hutton, Lucretius); the Talos cognition that treats the irreversible as merely deep — their fatal category error.
- [block-universe.md](block-universe.md) — the four-dimensional view in which the past is fixed; the fossil record is already written, and the Talos thread cannot end any other way.
- [cassandra.md](cassandra.md) — Aeschylus' seer cursed to be right and disbelieved; Asha's exact structural position.
- [ozymandias.md](ozymandias.md) — Shelley's emblem of hubris and erasure; the Belt wreckage and the centimeter of clay as the trunkless legs in the desert.
