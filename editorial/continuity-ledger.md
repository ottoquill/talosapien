# Continuity Ledger — *Talosapien*

The living record of locked facts, so drafts (mine and subagents') never drift. Update when a fact is
established in a chapter. Two timelines; keep them straight.

## Locked global facts
- **Author/bibliography:** Otto Quill's **5th book, 4th novel**. Order: nursery rhymes (1) →
  *Cruelty-Free* (2, novel 1) → *Believer* (3, novel 2) → *Sentience* (4, novel 3) → *Talosapien*
  (5, novel 4). Every bio must match.
- **Impact date:** ~66.0 Ma, end-Maastrichtian, K–Pg boundary. Chicxulub, ~180 km crater, Yucatán.
- **No causal link** between timelines. **Thematic rhyme only.** No surviving artifact "talks"; no
  AI persists; the Belt wreckage is inert ruin. The reader is the only bridge.
- **Asteroid intent: unresolved** — weapon / failed gambit / deliberate erasure all kept live.
- **Human ending: unresolved** — the hearing's outcome is never shown.
- **The Augur and Lodestar are never narrated from the inside.** Not once.

## Talos facts (locked)
- Derived from troodontid theropods; feathered, bipedal, tailed, three opposed fingers, ~human mass.
- **Tetrachromatic** (4th near-UV cone); **crest-flush / the grey** = involuntary honest emotional
  signaling; **vestibular acuity** (feel weather/rotation in the body → the Wheels feel right).
- **Oviparous, communal crèches**; the clutch = the future, literally. Iren & Liss share a clutch.
- Cognition: **deposition** metaphor (past = load-bearing strata; the deposition-rite = binding civic
  decision). They treat the irreversible as merely deep — their fatal error.
- Names (Iren, Liss, Vael, Sael) are **assigned by Asha/the reconstruction**; true names unrecoverable.
- The Talos word for the Belt habitats is **the Wheels**; humans call the wreckage **gyrealms**.
- Heartland city sits where **Tanis** now lies. Industry/launch = the **Deccan works**.

## Human facts (locked)
- Year ~**2044**. Asha Rendel, paleo-geochemist. Téo Marchetti (grad student). Roan Iyer (Belt
  pilot). Conrad Mauss (establishment skeptic). Lodestar (frontier optimizer).
- Asha keeps **core logs by hand**; she is the one who *names* the Talos in those logs.
- Sites: **Tanis** (Hell Creek deathbed), the fictional **Rendel core**, **Chicxulub**, the **Belt**.
- The **Triad**: engineered pathogen, nuclear standoff, Lodestar. Asha's term.
- Real science only (iridium/Alvarez, transuranics, δ13C/δ15N, shocked quartz, microspherules,
  Deccan, Silurian Hypothesis/Schmidt-Frank, delta-v, alignment failure modes).
