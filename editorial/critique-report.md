# Editorial Critique Report — *Talosapien*

A six-referee panel read the complete manuscript, each along one dimension, then findings were
triaged and resolved. Verdicts were uniformly strong; the issues were one continuity blocker, a
handful of majors, and minor/nit polish. This file records the panel's verdict and the resolution
log.

## Panel verdicts (summary)

- **Hard-SF science rigor:** "Genuinely award-grade and almost entirely clean." Decay-clock
  epistemics (it's the *ratio*, not the presence), the two-clock cross-check, shocked quartz, the
  Tanis seiche-surge, and the barely-plausible/underdetermined Belt redirect all judged correct and
  load-bearing. Alignment failure modes dramatized, never named; the Augur/Lodestar never narrated
  from inside. No physics errors; only calibration nits.
- **Paleontology & deep-time plausibility:** "Outstanding and very nearly bulletproof." Troodontid
  biology, Maastrichtian setting, and the Silurian-Hypothesis recoverability budget all disciplined.
- **Literary prose ("amazing for some"):** "Clears the bar — not occasionally but as a sustained
  achievement." The frame voice singled out as the book's triumph. Main notes: a few signature
  figures recur too often.
- **Continuity & structure:** "Exceptionally disciplined." One blocker (the clutch hatches twice)
  and one major (epoch-tag season order runs backwards); the structural rhyme is never stated
  outright except, by design, in the frame voice.
- **Premise failure-mode (dinosaur silliness / earned ambiguity):** "On the make-or-break axis this
  manuscript is, frankly, a triumph — not a single sentence would make a serious reader smirk." Both
  refusals (the asteroid's intent; the unshown hearing outcome) judged earned, not evasive.
- **Philosophy & the steel-man:** "Exemplary — the single best-executed version of this material I
  have read." Mauss genuinely wins on the page where he must; Vael's pro-delegation case persuasive.
  One important note: the real Schmidt–Frank paper was over-claimed as having *proved* unprovability.

## Resolution log (what was changed)

**Blocker**
- *Clutch hatches twice.* Ch15 and Ch17 referenced hatchlings though Ch19 ("Hatching") dramatizes
  the hatching. Reverted Ch15/Ch17 to eggs near hatching (precocial in-shell peeping kept the
  tenderness); Ch19 remains the singular hatching.

**Major**
- *Epoch-tag season order.* Retagged the deep-past chapters into a monotonic countdown — Part I "the
  last summer," Part II "the last autumn," Part III "the last winter," Part IV "the last day" — so
  the clutch's egg→hatch→hatchling arc runs forward.
- *Schmidt–Frank over-claim* (citation integrity, the book's one load-bearing real citation).
  Softened "they proved / the result is airtight / correct" to "argued / sound / the Schmidt–Frank
  problem" across Ch4, Ch20, Ch22, Ch25, Ch28 — the real paper raises the hypothesis and argues
  detection is hard; it does not prove unprovability.
- *Repeated somatic figure* ("the floor tilted by an amount too small for anyone but the frightened
  to feel"). Reserved for the Ch1 origin, the Ch21 Vael-apex, and the Ch26 impact; varied the Ch15
  and Ch17 instances.
- *Liss coda repetition* ("faith… not hope… not mistake it for it"). Reserved for Ch24 (its natural
  home, the last spring); varied the Ch11 and Ch19 closes.
- *Ch20 Lodestar exposition.* Compressed the doubled articulation of the isomorphism so it lands
  once, in its strongest image (the optimizer "wrong at you with a perfectly steady face").

**Minor / factual**
- Ch26: "sixty million stadia" (wrong distance) → "out past the fourth wanderer."
- Ch22: "older than the mammals" (backwards; mammals predate 66 Ma) → "predated the human species by
  tens of millions of years."
- Ch10: Asha's 2034 wound said "δ15N reading"; reconciled to the locked fact ("Carolina margin,
  Pliocene, trace-metal reading") to match Ch12 and the continuity ledger.
- "Small dishonesty of a man who…" refrain trimmed where it doubled with the floor-tilt figure.

**Deliberately left (licensed choices)**
- Ch29 leaning hardest on the reader-as-only-bridge device: licensed to the frame voice and the
  point of the chapter; the panel agreed it is within bounds.
- Ch16's withheld word "field" over the made soil: restrained and eerie by design, not silly.
- A few minor refrains (the smoke-alarm; "from the inside it was all"): within tolerance; the panel
  rated them nits and the prose elsewhere earns them.

## Strengths the panel flagged (do not touch)
The frame voice; the decay-clock and two-clock-cross-check epistemics; the shocked-quartz set-piece;
Sael's "balance that chooses which basket gets weighed" (specification gaming, dramatized); Mauss's
"base rate is a price, not a verdict"; the total physiological discipline of the Talos (no smirk in
the book); the two refusals to resolve, both earned; "there were four" as the cross-timeline hinge.
